About Us

At JunkForce, we make it easy to clear out your space—with respect for your time, your property, and the environment. Whether it’s one item or a full truckload, our team handles every job with care, caution, and the true spirit of Aloha.

♻️ Eco-Friendly and Community-Minded
We’re not your average junk haulers. Our mission is to reduce waste, not just move it. Whenever possible, we:

  • Reuse, repurpose, and salvage items to keep bulk out of landfills
  • Offer deep discounts when your load includes reusable bulk
  • Provide immediate purchase offers on select household contents, which can significantly reduce your junk removal cost

Our goal is to give usable items a second life while saving you money and making a positive impact.

✅ Safe, Insured, and Professional
Your peace of mind matters. We are:

  • Fully insured
  • Trained to work safely, cautiously, and cleanly
  • Dedicated to protecting the environment and the space we’re working in
  • Known for being punctual, reliable, and always going the extra mile

💲 Fair Pricing with Aloha
We offer some of the most affordable rates on the island, without sacrificing quality or service. We believe everyone deserves respectful, efficient junk removal—and that’s exactly what we deliver.

🤝 Flexible, Friendly, and Communicative
We believe in open communication and flexibility. Every job is unique, and we’re happy to make accommodations that suit your needs. Our goal is to leave every site clean, and every customer feeling like a friend.
